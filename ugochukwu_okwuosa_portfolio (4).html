Project Showcases

Case Studies

Real work completed during professional training — demonstrating the depth of execution behind every skill claimed.

Automation Medical Alert System — Zapier Workflow
The Challenge

A healthcare provider needed an automated system to send targeted email alerts when patients submitted specific responses on a Google Form — without any manual monitoring.

The Solution

Built a 3-step Zapier automation: Google Forms captures new responses → Filter by Zapier checks for trigger conditions → Gmail sends a customised alert email instantly.

Outcome

Zero-touch alert system deployed. The provider receives instant email notifications for flagged patient submissions — reducing response time from hours to seconds.

CRM HubSpot Patient Contact Management
The Challenge

Managing a healthcare contact record from intake to follow-up — including logging medication instructions, setting task reminders, and tracking lifecycle stage transitions.

The Solution

Configured a full HubSpot CRM record for patient "Daniel Chuks": logged a detailed medication email (Amlodipine, Lisinopril, Rosuvastatin), set medium-priority follow-up tasks, moved the contact to Lead stage, and used HubSpot's AI Contact Insights for suggested next actions.

Outcome

A clean, auditable patient record with activity history, upcoming tasks, and AI-generated engagement insights — ready for any team member to pick up seamlessly.

Travel Planning Business Travel Plan — Romania Trip
The Challenge

Create a complete business travel package for a 3-day trip to Romania, from data collection to a polished deliverable the executive could use directly.

The Solution

Built a multi-section Google Form to capture departure dates, destination, budget limits, preferred airline class, hotel type, dietary needs, and travel purpose. Designed a Canva travel plan document covering: Entry requirements, Flights, Accommodation, Must-see attractions, Local cuisine, Social norms, Budget breakdown, and Itinerary summary.

Outcome

Professional 11-page travel plan with branded design. The executive received everything needed for the trip in a single document — zero back-and-forth.

Admin & Scheduling Executive Calendar & Meeting Coordination
The Challenge

Coordinate a 1.5-hour onboarding meeting between a VA and a new client — booking, email communication, Google Meet setup, and calendar confirmation.

The Solution

Drafted a personalised outreach email with a Google Meet scheduling link, set up the appointment (Apr 8, 2026, 8–9:30am UTC), and confirmed via Gmail with full meeting details, cancel/reschedule options, and a test-your-setup link.

Outcome

Seamless, professional meeting coordination — the client received a complete booking confirmation with zero friction. Calendar block added automatically.

Documentation Healthcare Certificates Research Spreadsheet
The Challenge

Identify and document the key professional certifications a Healthcare Virtual Assistant should pursue, with provider details, focus areas, and cost benchmarks.

The Solution

Structured a Google Sheets database with Certificate, Provider, Focus, and Price columns — covering CMAA, CCMA, CMSA, HIPAA, and Medical Billing & Coding. Added a visualised Price Worth chart for quick comparative analysis.

Outcome

A ready-to-use reference document with visual chart — enabling informed, data-backed certification decisions for any aspiring healthcare VA.
