Automation · VAMedical Alert System — Zapier Workflow
Challenge

A healthcare provider needed automated email alerts when patients submitted flagged Google Form responses — with zero manual monitoring.

Solution

Configured a 3-step Zapier automation workflow connecting Google Forms → Filter by Zapier → Gmail — triggering condition-based email alerts on every new patient submission.

Outcome

Zero-touch alert system. Provider receives instant notifications for flagged responses — response time cut from hours to seconds.

CRM · Medical VAHubSpot Patient Contact Management
Challenge

Managing a full patient record from intake to follow-up — medication logging, task setting, and lifecycle tracking.

Solution

Managed a full HubSpot patient record — logging a medication instruction email (Amlodipine, Lisinopril, Rosuvastatin), setting medium-priority follow-up tasks, updating the contact to Lead stage, and utilising HubSpot's AI-powered contact insights for next-action recommendations.

Outcome

Clean, auditable patient record with full activity history and AI next-action suggestions — ready for any team member to continue.

Travel Planning · VABusiness Travel Plan — Romania 3-Day Trip
Challenge

Produce a complete business travel package for a 3-day Romania trip — from preference collection to a polished, ready-to-use deliverable.

Solution

Assembled a Google Form to collect all travel preferences, then produced an 11-page Canva travel plan document covering: entry requirements, flights, hotels, attractions, local cuisine, social norms, budget breakdown, and full itinerary.

Outcome

Professional branded travel plan. Executive had everything needed in one document — zero follow-up required.

Documentation · Medical VAHealthcare Certificates Research Spreadsheet

Identify and document key professional certifications for Healthcare VAs — with provider, focus, and cost data.

EHR · Medical VACarepatron — Client Database & Patient Management
Challenge

The Keva Health practice required a clean, well-organised client database within Carepatron — with accurate status tracking, patient tagging, and team assignment — all accessible and actionable at a glance.

Solution

Managed and organised the Carepatron client database for Keva Health — tagging 4 active patients by service type (Telehealth, Intake, Couple, Assessment, Group), updating lead/active/waitlist statuses, and assigning team members. Ensured accurate records and seamless filtering.

Outcome

A fully organised, filterable patient database — the clinic can locate any client by name, ID, tag, or status in seconds. Zero missed records.

Forms & Templates · Medical VACarepatron — Clinical Template Library (Keva Health)
Challenge

The clinic needed a structured library of reusable clinical forms and assessment templates — covering intake, consent, safety, mental health, and ADHD screening — customised for their practice.

Solution

Navigated and organised 12+ existing clinical templates within Carepatron — including Initial Clinical Assessment, Informed Consent for Minors, Patient Safety Forms, Standard Intake, Dissociative Experiences Scale, Adult ADHD Self-Report, Mental Status Exam, and more — categorising and tagging all under Keva Health for easy team access.

Outcome

A fully organised, accessible template library. Providers can locate and deploy any assessment or intake form in one click — saving hours of admin time per week.

Clinical Forms · Medical VACarepatron — Intake Assessment & Appointment Scheduling
Challenge

Healthcare providers needed a comprehensive, ready-to-use intake assessment form for new counselling clients — covering referral source, goals, mental health history, and risk screening.

Solution

Operated Carepatron's existing Intake Assessment template — thoroughly completing and managing all sections including referral source, reason for counselling, goals, self-harm history, suicidal ideation screening, family mental health history, and psychiatric hospitalisation history. Scheduled patient appointments (Sam Doxi — confirmed May 4, 2026, 9–10am) and managed the appointment notification pipeline via Carepatron and Gmail.

Outcome

A HIPAA-aware, clinically thorough intake system — every new patient is properly screened and booked with full audit trail and automated email confirmation.

Telehealth · Medical VACarepatron — Telehealth Video Session & Appointment Notifications
Challenge

A mental health clinic needed a reliable telehealth infrastructure — video consultations, secure messaging, and automated appointment notification emails for patients.

Solution

Operated Carepatron's built-in telehealth video system — conducting secure video sessions with AI Scribe enabled for session-to-notes automation, managed the secure in-app messaging module, and verified that appointment notification emails were correctly delivered to patients (e.g. Keva Health confirmed appointment, Sun May 3, 2026).

Outcome

A fully operational telehealth environment — video-ready, HIPAA-secure, with automated patient notifications and AI-assisted documentation. Zero technical friction for providers or patients.
